About Lagoon Paradise

Lagoon Paradise Resort
is one of the finest hospitality destinations on the Red Sea coast, where luxury meets the beauty of nature to offer visitors an exceptional experience of comfort and sophistication. The resort stands out with its unique location and stunning sea views, along with a diverse range of services and facilities that cater to families and individuals seeking an experience that combines relaxation with entertainment. Whether you're looking for a peaceful getaway, a luxurious stay with your family, or a special place to celebrate your finest moments — Lagoon Paradise offers all of this and more in an atmosphere filled with privacy and comfort. Our Location

Lagoon Paradise Resort is located within Durrat Al Arous, north of Jeddah on the Red Sea coast, approximately 45 minutes by car from downtown Jeddah via King Abdullah Coastal Road towards Dhahban.

The resort is part of the serene waterfront of Durrat Al Arous, combining upscale maritime atmosphere with family entertainment services in a safe and integrated environment.

Getting to the Resort

Visitors can easily reach us by private car or shuttle services via the coastal road leading to Dhahban, where clear signage directs you to the main gate of Durrat Al Arous.

After entering the gate, head towards the lagoon area where Lagoon Paradise Resort is located directly on the waterfront. The resort provides private parking for guests and visitors.
